How Povall works

To spread those pitiful lives to the world, the PoVall activates the function where some local or community organizations that aim to alleviate poverty can do some important things such as post their upcoming campaigns, call for donations from other kind-hearted people and invite volunteers from local citizens for their campaigns. This website is designed for organisations to manage their donations easily and safely as well as manage their members that are signed up for the campaigns.
